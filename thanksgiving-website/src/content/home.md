## What is Thanksgiving?

Thanksgiving is a national holiday celebrated in the United States and Canada. It originated as a harvest festival, where people would give thanks for the bountiful harvest and the blessings of the past year. Today, it is celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November in the U.S. and the second Monday of October in Canada.

## Traditions

Common traditions include:

- **Feasting**: Families gather to enjoy a meal that typically includes turkey, stuffing, cranberry sauce, and pumpkin pie.
- **Gratitude**: Many families take turns sharing what they are thankful for before the meal.
- **Parades**: Various cities host Thanksgiving parades, featuring floats, performances, and community spirit.
- **Football**: Watching football games is a popular pastime on Thanksgiving Day.

## Conclusion

Thanksgiving is a cherished holiday that brings people together to celebrate gratitude, family, and community. We hope this website helps you learn more about this wonderful tradition and inspires you to create your own Thanksgiving memories.
