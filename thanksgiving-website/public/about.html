About Thanksgiving

Thanksgiving is a national holiday celebrated in the United States and Canada. It is a time for family and friends to gather and give thanks for the harvest and the blessings of the past year.

The holiday has its origins in the early 17th century when English Pilgrims and Native Americans shared a harvest feast in 1621. This event is often regarded as one of the first Thanksgiving celebrations in the colonies.

Today, Thanksgiving is celebrated with a traditional meal that typically includes turkey, stuffing, and pumpkin pie. It is also a time for reflection, gratitude, and spending time with loved ones.
